Before activating your Text Bridge account, I need your permission to enroll you in the service. By enrolling in Text Bridge, you agree to receive service-related SMS communications through the Text Bridge system. Message frequency varies based on your usage. Message and data rates may apply. You may cancel your Text Bridge service at any time by contacting K Tel LLC. Do you agree to enroll in the Text Bridge service and receive these communications?

How Text Bridge Messages Work

Each subscriber receives a dedicated telephone number. Mobile phone users may voluntarily send SMS messages to the subscriber's assigned Text Bridge number. Text Bridge converts incoming SMS messages into voice prompts that the subscriber can hear by telephone.

Subscribers may dictate replies by telephone. Text Bridge converts the spoken reply into SMS text and sends it back to the original sender after the subscriber approves the reply.

No Marketing or Bulk Messaging

Text Bridge does not send unsolicited messages, promotional messages, marketing messages, or bulk messaging. Messages are conversational communications between subscribers and their family members, friends, customers, and other contacts.
